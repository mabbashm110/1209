What We Do

At IZ Consultancy Inc., we specialize in providing high-quality housing solutions for families in Rockford, Illinois. Our services include:

  • Property Rentals: We offer a range of rental properties suitable for families, ensuring comfortable and well-maintained homes.
  • Rent-to-Own Programs: We provide opportunities for families to transition from renting to homeownership through our rent-to-own options.
  • Real Estate Investment: We help W2 investors build passive income through lucrative rental properties.
  • Property Management: Our team handles all aspects of property management, ensuring a smooth experience for our tenants and investors.
  • Family-Focused Housing: We prioritize creating living spaces that cater to the needs of families, with a focus on safety, comfort, and convenience.
  • Consultation Services: We offer guidance and advice to families navigating the rental, home-buying, and real estate investment processes in Rockford.

Our mission is to help families and investors find their ideal real estate solutions and support them in their journey towards stable, long-term housing and financial growth. Whether you're looking to rent, considering a rent-to-own option, or want to invest in real estate, IZ Consultancy Inc. is here to assist you every step of the way.
