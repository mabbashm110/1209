Rent to Own Opportunities

At IZ Consultancy Inc., we offer rent-to-own options for families who are looking to transition from renting to homeownership. Our rent-to-own program allows you to rent a property with the option to purchase it in the future.

Benefits of Rent to Own:

  • Build equity while renting
  • Lock in a purchase price
  • Try out the home and neighborhood before committing to buy
  • Improve your credit score and save for a down payment

Contact us to learn more about our current rent-to-own opportunities and how you can get started on the path to homeownership.
