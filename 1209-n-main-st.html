About This Property

This spacious 5-bedroom, 2-bathroom home is located in the heart of Rockford, ideal for families seeking a comfortable rental property. The house features a car garage for secure parking, a balcony for outdoor relaxation, 3 spacious living rooms for various family activities, and 1 dining room for family meals and gatherings.

This layout provides plenty of space for family life and private activities. The monthly rent of $1,800 excludes utilities, allowing tenants to manage their own energy usage. Please note that this property is intended for single-family occupancy only, and subletting is not permitted.
